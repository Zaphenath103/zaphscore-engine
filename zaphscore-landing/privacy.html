Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026

What We Collect

  • Account data: Email, auth tokens (stored by Supabase)
  • Scan data: Repository URLs, scan results, scores
  • Usage data: Timestamps, API logs (90-day retention)
  • Payment data: Processed by Stripe — we never see card numbers

How We Use Your Data

  • Deliver scan results to your account
  • Improve scoring using anonymized aggregate patterns
  • Process billing via Stripe

Encryption at Rest

All scan findings are encrypted using Fernet AES-128-CBC before storage. Keys are stored separately from data.

Data Retention

  • Scan results: retained while account is active
  • API logs: 90 days
  • Billing records: 7 years (legal requirement)
  • Deleted accounts: all data purged within 30 days

Your Rights (GDPR)

  • Access: Request all data we hold on you
  • Delete: Permanent deletion via DELETE /api/user/me — cascades to all scans
  • Portability: Export scan history as JSON
  • Object: Opt out of aggregate analytics

Third Parties

  • Supabase — auth and database
  • Stripe — payments
  • Vercel / Railway — hosting

We do not sell data. We do not share individual scan results with any third party.

Silent Scanning

ZaphScore may scan public repos for aggregate metrics. Opt out by adding zaphscore: opt-out to your README.
